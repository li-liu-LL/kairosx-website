Jurisdictions

KairosX operates through jurisdiction-specific pathways.

Jurisdiction matters for financial infrastructure. KairosX presents Bhutan, Luxembourg, and future markets separately so stakeholders can understand status, scope, and public positioning without conflating corporate ambition with authorisation.

Current Structure

Each jurisdiction has its own role and status language.

KairosX

Corporate financial infrastructure identity.

KairosX is the public corporate identity for digital assets, payments, financial infrastructure, strategic investments, and emerging market opportunities.

Role

Corporate positioning and strategic coordination.

KairosX Bhutan

Bhutan Digital Economy Initiative.

KairosX Bhutan has received in-principle approval connected to the Bhutan Digital Economy Initiative. The initiative should be understood as a controlled implementation pathway.

Status language

In-principle approval; implementation remains subject to applicable conditions, final approvals, and operational readiness.

KairosX Luxembourg

European digital asset and payment infrastructure pathway.

KairosX Luxembourg is being prepared around European digital asset and payment infrastructure, including MiCA-related activity, subject to authorisation.

Status language

Subject to applicable authorisation, governance, safeguarding, compliance, and resilience requirements.

Future Jurisdictions

Additional markets are evaluated through substance, not only opportunity.

KairosX may evaluate further jurisdictions where financial infrastructure can be supported by legal clarity, banking connectivity, responsible counterparties, public-sector fit, and a credible operating model.

No future market should be read as active or authorised until KairosX can describe its status accurately and publicly. That discipline is important for banks, regulators, vendors, and partners who need precise information before engagement.

Public Positioning

The website is intentionally careful with regulatory language.

KairosX avoids public overstatement. References to approvals, MiCA-related activity, digital asset services, payment activity, and market entry are always subject to the relevant jurisdiction, authorisation status, conditions, and implementation readiness.

KairosX is a financial infrastructure company focused on digital assets, payments, strategic investments, and emerging market opportunities.
