Privacy & Legal

How KairosX handles information and describes its activities.

This notice explains what the KairosX corporate website does with information, how messages are handled, and the basis on which regulatory and jurisdiction statements are made. It applies to this website only and does not describe the data practices of any KairosX operating entity under separate authorisation.

Information We Handle

The website itself collects as little as possible.

KairosX treats restraint with data as part of institutional discipline. The website is a static information site and is not designed to track visitors or build profiles.

Contact

Messages are sent by email, not stored on this site.

The contact form prepares an email from your own mail application addressed to a KairosX inbox. The information you choose to include — such as your name, organisation, email, and message — is handled as ordinary business correspondence and used only to respond to your enquiry.

Cookies

No advertising or tracking cookies are set.

This website does not use advertising cookies or third-party tracking. Any future use of strictly necessary or analytics technologies will be described here before it is introduced.

Hosting

Standard server logs may be retained for security.

Like most websites, the hosting environment may record technical access logs (such as IP address, timestamp, and requested page) for security, integrity, and operational purposes. These are not used to identify visitors for marketing.

Your Choices

Requests about your information.

If you have contacted KairosX and wish to ask what correspondence is held, request a correction, or ask that it be deleted, you can write to the contact inbox below. KairosX will handle such requests in line with applicable data protection obligations in the relevant jurisdiction.
